## Features Implemented
1. Tinder-style swipe cards with spring physics, rotation, haptics
2. Pinch-to-zoom on cards (1x-5x), double-tap to reset
3. Smart album sources: All Photos, Screenshots, Videos, Selfies, Live Photos, Favorites
4. On This Day, Random, By Month album sources
5. Batch deletion safety (queue → review → confirm)
6. Session + lifetime stats tracking
7. Onboarding flow (3 pages, animated with parallax)
8. Settings (sort order, swipe sensitivity, haptics toggle, card style)
9. Supercut video generator:
   - Vision AI photo scoring (sharpness, faces, saliency)
   - AVFoundation video composition with crossfades
   - Beat-matched music sync
   - Music picker (Apple Music, Files upload, Pixabay free library)
   - Progress view + preview/save/share
10. Video cards show play button + duration badge
11. Context menu "Create Supercut" on all album cards

- Swipe delay → callback fires immediately, animation concurrent
- Photos not loading → fixed weak self capture in loadSource
